About StartUp

8 years ago, StartUp was founded in SCAD Hong Kong between a small group of 15 students. Today, StartUp is a multi-campus design competition that is created by students, for students.

Grab your friends from across majors and create a dream team! Wait for the theme to drop at Kickoff and create your best project yet.

Frequently Asked Questions

When are sign ups?
Sign ups are OPEN! Sign up solo or grab your friends!

What do I need to participate?
Just you and your teams’ (if you have one!) student info: SCAD student ID numbers, SCAD emails, majors, and year. There is no experience needed to participate. It’s optional, but we also recommend adding your portfolio and resume so we can send it off to our industry guests!

Do I need a team to sign up?
Nope! You can sign up alone, and we’ll group you together with other solos! StartUp is a fantastic way to work with other majors. If you find a team after signing up individually, no worries! Send a message over to scadstartup@gmail.com and we’ll take care of you.

Want to contact us?
Feel free to reach out at any time and we'll get back to you ASAP!
Email: scadstartup@gmail.com
Instagram: @scadstartup

‍How many students on a team?
3-5 students per team.

‍Who is eligible to sign up?
Savannah, Atlanta, and SCADnow students.

Is StartUp 2023 on-ground?
Kickoff and Finals are going to be hosted in-person at Arnold Hall and the SCAD Museum of Art, respectively. Atlanta students will have simultaneous hosts in the Training Rooms and DMC Theatre.

What’s the theme?
You’ll see ;)

Is StartUp earlier this year?
Yes! StartUp is happening from Friday of WEEK 3 to Friday of WEEK 4.
